Frequently Asked Questions
Manage the FAQ content shown to parents
Question Answer Actions
How do I place an order? Log in to your parent account, navigate to the Order section, select your child's school, and choose from the available menu items. Orders can be placed up to the weekly cutoff time.
What is the order cutoff time? Orders must be submitted by 8:00 PM on Sunday for the following week's lunches. Late orders cannot be accommodated as food is prepared fresh each morning.
Can I change or cancel an order? Yes — orders can be modified or canceled up to the Sunday 8:00 PM cutoff. After that time, orders are locked and sent to the kitchen. Please contact us directly for emergencies.
Are allergen-free options available? We offer gluten-free, nut-free, and dairy-free options on many menu items. Allergen information is displayed on each menu item. Please contact us if your child has severe allergies.
How is payment processed? We accept all major credit and debit cards via our secure payment portal. Payments are charged when your order is confirmed. We do not store card details on our servers.
What happens on school holidays? No lunches are delivered on school holidays or snow days. Our system automatically blocks ordering for those days. If your school has an unexpected closure, please call us before 7:00 AM for a credit.
